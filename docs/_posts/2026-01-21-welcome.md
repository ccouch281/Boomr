Welcome to the official development log for **Boomr**. This blog will serve as a central hub for updates, technical deep dives, and behind-the-scenes looks at the development of my Unreal Engine 4 project.

## Why a Dev Blog?
Game development is a journey of constant learning and iteration. By documenting my process, I hope to:

*   Keep track of my progress and decisions.
*   Share knowledge with the broader game dev community.
*   Maintain a history of my technical decisions.

## The Vision: Arena meets Tactical
My goal is to create a First Person Shooter that blends properties of arena-style shooters with tactical gameplay, very much in the vein of *Halo: Combat Evolved*.

I've been disappointed in the official *Halo 1* remakes and spiritual successors over the years. They never quite capture the magic of the original. I'm hoping to build something that finally fills that voidâ€”a game that respects the golden era of shooters while feeling fresh and modern.

## Call for Contributors
I am a programmer first and foremost. While I can handle the code and gameplay logic, I have gaps in my skillset that I'll need help filling.

If you are interested in this vision and have skills in:

*   **3D Modeling & Art** (Characters, Environments, Weapons)
*   **Level Design** (Mapping, Flow, Item Placement)
*   **Animation** (Rigging, Gameplay Animations)
*   **Sound Design** (SFX, Music)
*   **UI/UX Design** (HUD, Menus)
*   **Server Architecture & Networking**

Please reach out or check the GitHub repository issues. I'd love to collaborate with others who share this passion for the classic tactical-arena hybrid genre.

## Getting Help: The Power of AI
I plan to write tutorials and quickstart guides as we progress, but if you run into specific problems or need to learn a new concept quickly, I highly recommend using an AI assistant. They are infinitely better at providing immediate, context-aware help than I could ever be. Best of all, they are all largely free to use.
